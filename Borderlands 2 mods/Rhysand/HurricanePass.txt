    The name references the highest mountain pass in Colorado, USA: Hurricane Pass. 
    EXECUTE AFTER COMMUNITY PATCH.
    Elementally unlocks Commerce - If you forget to execute the mod before loading a save the gun will be deleted.
    Fires a vertical pattern of 8 unlisted projectiles.
    On Impact: Spawns Swordsplosion swords, ChereAmie healing projectiles, and Land mines. They all deal no self damage.
    Strong but not overpowered without Bee. STUPIDLY overpowered when used with the Bee (at or above the level of a Sandhawk).
    +115% critical damage, +Slowish Ammo Regeneration, 2 ammo per shot.
    Comes with a snowy custom skin.
    Replaces Commerce - Farmed from Assassin Wot in Southpaw Steam & Power/Digi Peak after the mission Assassinate the Assassins.
    Perfect Gibbed Code: BL2(hwAAAACYgQBCyANBDAGFdmI5wdKMZQGLCpYGLBVY/v8rYyHCgo3l) - Fire.
    By Rhysand.
