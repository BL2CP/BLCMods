    EXECUTE THIS FILE *AFTER* THE COMMUNITY PATCH AND ANY OTHER MODS THAT CHANGE THE PITCHFORK, OR EXECUTE IT LAST TO BE SAFE.
    The Court of Nightmares is meant to directly compete with the Court of Dreams.
    The snipers will have identical stats when they are the same level with perfect parts.
    Perfect Parts for CoD: Maliwan Grip, Dahl Stock, Dahl Sight, Banbury/Fire rate Prefix.
    Perfect Parts for CoN: Dahl Grip, Dahl Stock, Dahl Sight, Pacifying/Damage Prefix.
    It is quite powerful, but remember that this sniper is meant to compete with the Court of Dreams/Pimpernel, and is balanced as such.
    Fires in a circular, sun-like pattern; 5 additional projectiles split off and explode after a moment.
    The 5 explosions benefit from grenade damage boosts, the main projectile does not.
    It is better to not use this with high levels of Vel0city or any other skills that massively increase projectile speed.
    Replaces the Pitchfork - Can only be farmed from Terramorphous the Invincible. I suggest gibbed for this one.
    Perfect Gibbed Code: BL2(hwAAAAAfKQCBiINBBAGFdoJJAIMKZgXMBBgbMGRg/v+ngmEEcwvm) - Fire.
    By Rhysand.
