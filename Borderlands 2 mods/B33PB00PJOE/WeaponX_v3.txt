Mod by B33P B00P JOE

***WARNING***
Thanks to Orudeon, there is an optional code to have this always spawn with a bayonet on slot two.
USE THE FILTER TOOL TO TURN THIS OFF OR ON!!!
*************


The Weapon X replaces the Triqueta Shotgun with a legendary Shotgun based on the fan-favorite X-Man: Wolverine!
While the gun's claw pattern is decent enough, the added melee damage, and mutant Healing Factor really lets you ump into the fray for a berserker barrage!

+Shoots a new "Claw" firing pattern
+Optional Bayonet set to always spawn on slot 2
+Mutant Healing Factor grants you slight damage resistance (adamantium bones) on your HP bar only, as well as continual HP regeneration
+Bonus Melee damage and slight Movement speed boost
+Features a new paint job

Changes in V3:
Reformatted Text/Code

Changes in V2:
+Now works with the Order Shield (Feral Rage)


#</Description>
