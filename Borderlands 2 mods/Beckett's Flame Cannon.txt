by kunelsanders
Replaces the Flayer/Tidal Wave.
Meant to emulate Beckett's flame cannon from Gigantic.
Locked to the Huntin' accessory for as much accuracy as possible. It's supposed to be a longer-ranged weapon, not an in-your-face shotgun.
Also experiences no accuracy loss after a shot. Recoil is still a thing, though.
